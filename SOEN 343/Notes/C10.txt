Visibility

    - ability of an object to see
    - receiver must be visible to sender
    - 4 common ways:
        1. Attribute
            B is attribute of A
            permanent visibility 
        2. Parameter
            B is parameter of method A
            temporary visibility
        3. Local
            B is local object in method A
            temporary visibility
        4. Global 
            B is global to A
            permanent visibility
